Peer-to-Peer Wager Protocol. Verified. Immutable. Human-Powered.

The next-gen layer for social betting — decentralized, trackable, and frictionless.

Betya is a peer-to-peer wager protocol that documents, timestamps, and verifies friendly challenges — without holding funds. It acts as a trustless layer between users, providing a structured wager ledger, outcome verification flow, and automated settlement reminders. No middlemen. No forgotten bets. Just clear terms, mutual accountability, and a better way to settle the score.

Simple Flow. Solid Structure. No Loose Ends.

1. Create the Wager
Set your terms, opponent, deadline, and stakes — all in one clean form.

2. Lock It In
Both users confirm the wager to make it official. All terms are time-stamped and logged.

3. Complete the Challenge
Once the event or challenge ends, each user confirms the outcome.

4. Settle the Score
Betya sends a smart reminder to pay up — no excuses, no confusion.

Too Many Bets Are Lost in the Noise.

Handshake wagers and text thread bets are easy to forget, dispute, or dodge.

Informal bets get messy. People forget the terms. Screenshots go missing. One side ghosts. There’s no structure. No record. No easy way to prove who won or what was agreed. That’s where Betya comes in — a lightweight layer of accountability that formalizes every friendly wager with digital receipts, confirmation flows, and no room for "I don’t remember."

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Betya a gambling app? +
No. Betya is a peer-to-peer wager protocol for friendly, skill-based challenges. It does not facilitate or process gambling or chance-based bets.
How does Betya make money? +
Betya may charge a small fee for services such as wager verification or future escrow handling.
Do I need to link my payment accounts? +
No. Users log wager terms and are reminded to pay each other manually through their preferred payment apps.
What happens if someone doesn’t pay? +
Betya will log this on their profile and notify both users. Future versions may include reputation systems or dispute support.
Can I cancel a wager after it’s confirmed? +
Once both parties confirm, it is logged and considered binding. Both must agree to cancel.
How are outcomes verified? +
Both users submit the result. If there’s a mismatch, Betya initiates a confirmation flow or dispute sequence.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Betya uses Firebase authentication and access rules to protect user data.
Do I need to be in a specific state to use Betya? +
Betya is compliant with peer-to-peer, skill-based wager rules. Availability may vary based on local laws.
